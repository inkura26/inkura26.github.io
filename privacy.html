Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-04

1. Data We Process

  • Local reading data, such as bookshelf, progress, and theme settings.
  • iCloud sync data, only when you explicitly enable iCloud sync.
  • Purchase verification data, including transaction and device identifiers for anti-fraud checks.
  • Minimal security event data when high-risk runtime anomalies are detected.

2. What We Do Not Do

  • No cross-app or cross-site ad tracking.
  • No sale of personal data to advertisers.
  • No third-party ads inside the app.

3. Data Sharing & Storage

Reading data is not used for advertising. Any required verification/security processing is strictly limited to app functionality and safety.

4. Your Choices

  • You can disable iCloud sync in system/app settings.
  • You can clear app data in-app or uninstall the app to remove local data.

5. Contact

Email: privacy@inkura.app
