FAQ

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app and go to the Premium/Subscription page, then tap “Restore Purchases”.

iCloud sync is not working. What should I check?

Make sure iCloud is enabled on your device and iCloud sync is turned on inside Inkura settings.

Import failed for a source file. Why?

Please verify the source URL is reachable and the JSON format is valid.
